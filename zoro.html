Roronoa Zoro

What is Zoro known for?

Also known as "Pirate Hunter Zoro", he is a pirate, former bounty hunter from the anime and manga series One Piece. He is one of the most versatile fighters in the series and one of the strongest four fighters in the Straw Hat Pirates beside Monkey D.
